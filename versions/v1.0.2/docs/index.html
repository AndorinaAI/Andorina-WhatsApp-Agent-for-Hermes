Let your WhatsApp Fly

Andoriña gives wings to your Hermes agent. Forget typing: schedule messages, send voice notes, share PC files, and search your contacts instantly. Messaging on autopilot.

State-of-the-Art Technology

🔍

Google Contacts

Full sync with your account. Its smart search finds whoever you're looking for even if you make typos or forget accents.

📁

PC Files

Secure management of docs and multimedia from your PC. Intelligent local folder navigation with absolute protection against external access.

📥

Local Inbox

Persistent log of received messages. Allows Hermes to read the context and respond intelligently when you're away.

🎙️

Audio as Voice Notes

Convert any audio file (MP3, WAV, etc.) into a real Voice Note that is received with the native WhatsApp player.

⏰

Agenda & Cron

Smart message scheduling with integrated calendar support via the Hermes cron system.

🎯

Reliability Score

Intelligent decision engine. If contact certainty is over 80% (or your custom threshold), it sends the message automatically.

🛡️

Shield & Auto-patching

Firewall against attacks and "Self-Healing" system that automatically re-applies your features after each Hermes update.
