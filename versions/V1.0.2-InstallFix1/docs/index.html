Turn Hermes into an autonomous WhatsApp manager

Turn Hermes into an autonomous WhatsApp manager and take full control of your messaging. Forget typing: schedule messages, send voice notes, share PC files, and search your contacts instantly. Your WhatsApp, on total autopilot.

View on GitHub View all features

State-of-the-Art Technology

Google Contacts

Full sync with your account. Its smart search finds whoever you're looking for even if you make typos or forget accents.

PC Files

Automate sending and receiving files (they download directly to your PC). Ask your agent from anywhere to find a file on your PC and send it to you via WhatsApp.

Local Inbox

Persistent log of received messages. Allows Hermes to read the context and respond intelligently when you're away.

Audio as Voice Notes

Convert any audio file (MP3, WAV, etc.) into a real Voice Note that is received with the native WhatsApp player.

Agenda & Cron

Smart message scheduling now powered by native Linux crontab. Scheduled messages are sent instantly and without delay.

Reliability Score ⚠️ Disabled (Bug)

Intelligent decision engine. If contact certainty is over 80% (or your custom threshold), it sends the message automatically.

Shield & Auto-patching

Firewall against attacks and "Self-Healing" system that automatically re-applies your features after each Hermes update.
